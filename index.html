About

Dr. Roger Haene, Orthopaedic Foot & Ankle Surgeon

MB BCh, MRCS (Edin), FRCS (Tr&Orth), CCT (UK)

Assistant Professor (Dalhousie University in Halifax and Memorial University in St. Johns, Canada)

Consultant Orthopaedic and Trauma Surgeon, expert in adult foot & ankle surgery.

Asst. Prof. Dr. Roger Haene has a combined 19 years of experience in Orthopaedic Surgery. He trained in Orthopaedics in Cambridge, UK, leading to full CCT. Fellowships in Foot & Ankle Reconstruction followed in Toronto, Canada and Dublin, Ireland.

He then worked as a Consultant in Canada for 7 years, with 3 of those years working as Head of Department and Head of Orthopaedic Education in his hospital. He was awarded Assistant Professor Status by 2 Universities (Dalhousie University in Halifax and Memorial University in St. Johns). He was an executive committee member of the Canadian Orthopaedic Foot and Ankle Society.

He moved to the UAE over 5 years ago and has since worked in renowned government and private hospitals. He provides a complete adult Foot & Ankle reconstructive service, including treatment of trauma, sports injuries, deformities, arthritis and tendon transfers for neuromuscular conditions.

He is an executive member of the Emirates Foot & Ankle Club under the Emirates Medical Association.

He is frequently consulted as an expert by the Abu Dhabi Department of Health regarding International Patient Care.

He has published in respected orthopaedic scientific journals (listed here), is a regular speaker at international conferences, and is up-to-date with the latest evidence-based knowledge and surgical techniques.
